Frequently Asked Questions

What is ProConDuck about?

ProConDuck is a website designed around the idea of centralizing the best product deals and reviews to simplify your shopping experience. Read more about ProConDuck here.

How do I search for products?

To search for products, click on the products section in the header. From that page, you can filter the products shown by category and sort them based on your preferences. Click on a product that interests you to find reviews, deals, and other information about the product.

Can I trust reviews posted on ProConDuck?

Yes, you can definitely trust reviews posted on ProConDuck. Reviewers are consumers just like you and have complete creative control over the content of their posts. By filtering reviews and products through administrators, we maintain the best quality content. To learn more about the ethics of ProConDuck, see our Ethics Statement.

How can I become a reviewer?

If you would like to be a reviewer, don't be shy to contact us. We are always looking for skilled reviewers to contribute to the website.

Where can I ask other questions?

Send us your other questions here. We look forward to hearing from you and will get back to you in one to three business days.
